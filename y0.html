When choosing a career path in school, you can consider things like:

  • Interests: Consider what you enjoy doing, such as subjects you studied in high school or activities you enjoy in your free time.
  • Skills and strengths: Think about the skills and abilities you want to use and grow in.
  • Values: Consider what's important to you, such as financial security, work-life balance, or social justice.
  • Job market: Research the job market, demand for specific professions, and salary expectations.
  • Education and training: Consider the education and training requirements for different careers.
  • Personality: Consider your personality, as there are personality tests that can help you determine a career path.
  • Gain experience: Try to gain practical experience through an internship, volunteer work, or part-time job.
  • Talk to people: Talk to people who work in different fields to learn what they do and what it takes to succeed.
  • Use online resources: Use online resources like career assessments and job search sites.
  • Seek support: Seek support from your high school counselor.
